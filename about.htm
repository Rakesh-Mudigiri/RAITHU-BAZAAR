Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Farm & Family

Raithu Bazaar was born from a simple truth: Indian farmers deserve better. When farmers sell through middlemen, they get only 20-30% of the retail price. We're changing that.

Our platform connects farmers directly with families, ensuring farmers earn 2x more while customers get fresh, chemical-free produce at 40% less than retail prices.

Our Story

"We started Raithu Bazaar in Hyderabad after watching our farming families struggle with middlemen who took 60-70% of the produce value. A tomato that costs ₹40/kg in the market would earn the farmer just ₹8/kg."

"Our technology connects farmers directly with urban families. Today, our farmers earn 2x more, our customers pay 40% less, and the produce reaches homes within 24 hours of harvest — fresher than anything in the market."

"మా రైతులు సంతోషంగా ఉన్నారు. మా కుటుంబాలు ఆరోగ్యంగా ఉన్నాయి. ఇదే మా విజయం."

– Team Raithu Bazaar, Hyderabad
